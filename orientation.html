OJT WEEKLY BLOG

Orientation

February 20, 2026 marked our first meeting and the official start of our On-the-Job Training (OJT) at the Department of Health (DOH) Bicol Center for Health Development (CHD). We were oriented by Mrs. Anda, who provided us with important information regarding workplace policies, procedures, and expectations that we needed to follow during our training period.

After the orientation, we were assigned to our respective departments. I was assigned to the Regional Directors Office; however, since my designated mentor was not present at that time, I was temporarily assigned to the Office of the Assistant Regional Director for the day.

My initial task involved receiving various documents from the Records Section, such as emails, letters, purchase requests, and other official documents. After receiving these documents, I forwarded them to the Management Support Division (MSD), as they were the assigned Officer-in-Charge (OIC) for that day. I also processed and forwarded the said documents using the Document Tracking Information System (DTRAK) to ensure proper documentation, monitoring, and workflow of incoming and outgoing communications within the office.
